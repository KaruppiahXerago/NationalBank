Our Story

Founded in 1985, First National Bank has been serving our community for nearly four decades. What started as a small community bank has grown into a trusted financial institution serving thousands of customers.

We believe in building lasting relationships with our customers and providing personalized banking solutions that meet their unique financial needs.

Our commitment to financial excellence and community service has been the foundation of our growth and success.
